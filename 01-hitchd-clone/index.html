Frequently Asked Questions

What is a honeymoon registry?

A honeymoon registry is a type of wedding registry that allows couples to ask for honeymoon-related experiences and gifts, such as airfare, accommodation, and activities. Rather than asking guests for traditional gifts, like toasters or blenders, a honeymoon registry asks guests to give money towards experiences, activities and costs related to your honeymoon.

How is Hitchd different from other registries?

Other registries focus on traditional gifts, partnerships with vendors, and upselling couples or guests. Hitchd has one vision in mind, provide couples and guests with a world-class experience and focus on creating memories over material gifts.

Will my guests think it's tacky?

Times have changed. Contributing to your honeymoon fund makes guests feel like they're contributing to something meaningful, and guests can even personalize their cash contribution with a personalized message to wish you on your way.
